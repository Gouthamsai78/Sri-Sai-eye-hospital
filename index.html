The Problem

  • Creators struggle with inconsistent monetization beyond ads and sponsorships
  • Fans lack meaningful ways to financially support and invest in creators
  • Existing platforms offer limited engagement and no financial upside

The Solution

  • Flic offers a stock market where fans buy/sell shares of creators
  • Real-time creator metrics dynamically affect share prices
  • Gamified experience building engaged creator-fan communities
  • Roadmap includes blockchain tokenization for real-money trading

How It Works

  • Users receive virtual Flic coins to trade creator shares
  • Share prices fluctuate based on creator performance (views, growth, engagement)
  • Portfolio tracking and leaderboards drive engagement
  • YouTube API integration provides real-time data
  • Supply and demand dynamics create market sentiment
